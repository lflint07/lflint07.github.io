  • person_pinAbout Me

    I am an aspiring web developer currently living in the "Queen City", Charlotte, North Carolina. I was born in Staunton, Virginia where I lived my entire life until I attended Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (Virginia Tech). I graduated from Tech in 2011 with a business degree in Hospitality and Tourism Management. After graduation I moved back home and started the career adventure that I've been on ever since. I've worked in construction, in retail, sold voluntary benefit insurance, prepared taxes, reviewed financial contracts and worked as a digital projectionist for a movie theater. Every step of my journey has led me to my current position as a junior web developer. In my free time, I enjoy weightlifting and nutrition, watching tv shows and movies, and painting.

  • webWeb Development

    I have always had a vested interest in computers and technology but I never really considered the possibility of pursuing a career in the industry until I discovered Tech Talent South. TTS changed my perception of what I could accomplish and taking the full time code immersion boot camp has honestly (all joking aside) changed my life. After I decided to take the plunge I realized that there was so much I could learn and how real of an option a career in web development was. TTS has been a great way for me to get my career started while also building out my professional network and portfolio.

  • assessmentSkills

    During my code immersion bootcamp, I learned a variety of web development and software skills. The class primarily focused on the language Ruby and the framework Rails. The MVP methodology was utilized in the creation of all the applications. I became proficient in HTML and CSS, learned the basics of JavaScript, and was introduced to Bootstrap and Materialize. Through Tech Talent South, I was enrolled in a "Big Data" class as well. During that course, I learned about the role of big data and how it is being implemented in the industry today. I was introduced to the data driven tools Graph Story, Neo4J, and Cloud9 besides others.
